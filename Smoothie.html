Smoothie

A refreshing smoothie recept that will shock you. Deliciour and easy to make even for beginners!

Ingredients:

  • Milk (500ml)
  • 2 bananas
  • 1tbsp Peanut butter
  • Oats(100ml)
  • Ice cream(200ml)

Instructions:

  1. Put milk in the blender

  2. Put the 2 bananas along the milk in the blender

  3. together put the peanut butter and oats in the blender

  4. Lastly put the ice cream and turn on the blender

  5. Let it blend until it becomes a smooth liquid
